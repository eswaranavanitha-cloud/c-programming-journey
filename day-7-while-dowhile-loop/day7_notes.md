# Day 7 - Loops (while, do while, break, continue)

## Topics Covered
- while loop
- do while loop
- break statement
- continue statement

---

## while loop
Executes only if condition is true.

---

## do while loop
Executes at least once, then checks condition.

---

## break
Stops the loop immediately.

---

## continue
Skips current iteration and continues next loop cycle.

---

## Key Difference

break → stops loop  
continue → skips current step
